Our Team

At Sequlite Genomics, we're on a mission to democratize sequencing and make genomic insights accessible to all. Our team comprises passionate individuals dedicated to innovation, collaboration, and driving meaningful impact in the world of genomics.

Why Join Us?

  • Opportunity to contribute to cutting-edge research and development in sequencing technology.
  • Collaborative and dynamic work environment that encourages innovation.
  • Commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion.
  • Competitive compensation and benefits packages.
  • Career growth and development opportunities.
  • Free snacks!

How to Apply

To apply for any of the positions listed herein, please email your resume and a cover letter to careers@sequlite.com. Include the job title in the subject line.

We're Growing!

We usually post job openings at LinkedIn. Can't find a suitable opening? We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team. Send us your resume, and we'll keep it on file for future opportunities.

Internship Opportunities

We offer internships for students looking to gain hands-on experience in genomics and sequencing. Stay tuned for internship openings or email us your resume.
